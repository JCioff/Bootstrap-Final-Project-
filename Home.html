About Me

All work on this site was created and is owned by me. A number of days were spent compiling the work itself as well as framing the architechture for it to properly function. The site was designed as a portfolio of my work I have completed over the course of two semesters at James Madison University. In completing this, I have continued learning about the functionality and capabilities of multiple different applications as well as coding languages (CSS, HTML, Javascript, & Bootstrap).
